Two cafes, one roof

A coffee shop and a tea house, stitched together.

In April 2021, Thippi Luangpraseuth bought Global Grounds and folded her downtown tea shop, Dim Sum, into it. The pandemic had hammered both small businesses, and combining the two was how she kept them alive.

You can taste the merger on the menu. Locally roasted coffee from Bean Juice on one side, more than 80 loose leaf teas on the other, and a whole lot of plants hanging from the ceiling.
