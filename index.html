No. 01   Our Two Cafes

A coffee shop and a tea house, stitched together on State Street.

In April 2021, Thippi Luangpraseuth bought Global Grounds and folded her downtown Dim Sum Tea Shop into it. The pandemic had hammered both small businesses. Combining them was how she kept them alive.

You can taste the merger on the menu. Locally roasted coffee from Bean Juice on one side, more than 80 loose leaf teas on the other, boba for anyone who came in with a craving. The plants followed. The art did too.

No. 02   On the Bar

A few drinks the regulars come back for.

Coffee pulled on beans roasted a mile away. Matcha whisked by hand. Cold brew steeped slow. Teas from a wall of jars Thippi brought over from Dim Sum. Everything honest, nothing fussy.

  • Iced Lavender Matcha

    Signature

  • Salted Cream Cold Brew

  • Iced Caramel Macchiato

    Classic

    Double shot, vanilla, a slow ribbon of caramel over ice.

  • PB Banana Espresso

No. 03   The Tea Wall

80 plus loose leaf teas, brought over from Dim Sum Tea Shop in 2021.

Black, green, herbal, rooibos, boba, specialty. The chalkboard rotates, the jars stay full, and Amanda will walk you through any of it if you ask.
