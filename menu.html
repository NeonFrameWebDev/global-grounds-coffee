Tea

80 plus loose leaf

The tea wall came from Dim Sum Tea Shop when it merged into Global Grounds in 2021. What follows is just a taste of the categories.

B

Black

English Breakfast, Earl Grey, Assam, Darjeeling, plus a few smoky single origins.

G

Green

Sencha, Dragon Well, Gunpowder, Jasmine Pearls, plus a ceremonial-grade matcha we use in the latte.

H

Herbal & Rooibos

Chamomile, Peppermint, Lavender, Lemon Ginger, Vanilla Rooibos. Caffeine free.

B

Boba

Pick any tea, add tapioca pearls, choose your milk. The Dim Sum hold-over and a fast favorite.

S

Specialty

Pu-erh, Oolong, White Peony, seasonal blends. Ask and we will brew to your taste.

The full list lives on the chalkboard inside the shop. Our staff loves to talk tea, so walk in and ask what is freshest.
