Our
Story.

A coffee shop and a tea house, stitched together on State Street in La Crosse.

No. 01   The Merger

Two small La Crosse businesses. One small La Crosse save.

Global Grounds opened on State Street in 2010. A few years later, Thippi Luangpraseuth opened her Dim Sum Tea Shop downtown at 221 Pearl Street, built around an 80 plus loose leaf tea wall and a deep love of boba.

In 2020 the pandemic hit both businesses hard. In April 2021, Thippi took over Global Grounds and folded Dim Sum into the shop on State Street. Coffee on one side, the tea wall on the other, and a room full of plants between them.

Alina Piotrowski, who owned Global Grounds from 2019 to 2021, stayed on as General Manager through the transition and still runs the floor.

The pandemic really hit small businesses hard and in order to keep going, we had to think creatively.
Thippi Luangpraseuth, Owner

No. 02   Timeline

2010

Global Grounds opens.

A coffee shop goes in at 1808 State Street under its original owners. Locally roasted beans, a few couches, a window seat that would later become famous.

2019

Alina and Jorge take over.

Alina Piotrowski and Jorge Galván buy Global Grounds and keep the soul of the place intact while slowly upgrading the bar.

2021

The merger.

April 1. Thippi Luangpraseuth buys Global Grounds and moves her downtown Dim Sum Tea Shop into it. The 80 plus loose leaf teas and the boba line show up on State Street. Alina stays on as GM.

2026

Still here.

A coffee bar on one end, a tea wall on the other, more plants every week. Same door, same State Street, same commitment to brewing it locally.

No. 03   What Makes Us, Us

01

Local Beans, Bean Juice Coffee Co.

Every cup of coffee is pulled on beans roasted a mile up the road in small batches. Shorter supply chain, fresher espresso, a local relationship that shows up in the cup.

02

The Tea Wall.

More than 80 loose leaf teas came over from Dim Sum Tea Shop. Black, green, herbal, rooibos, boba, specialty. Amanda will walk you through any of it.

03

Plants, Art, Repeat.

Hanging plants live everywhere. Cultural art on the walls. The room changes a little every week because Thippi cannot leave well enough alone.

04

A Sit In Cafe.

Not grab and go. Bring a book, pull up a leather chair, use the free Wi-Fi, stay as long as your cup lasts. We want you to linger.
