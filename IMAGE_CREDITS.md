# Image Credits, Global Grounds Coffee Speculative Site

Every image on this site came from a real, public Global Grounds Coffee web property. No stock imagery, no AI-generated images.

## From their Facebook profile

Source: Facebook Graph public picture endpoint for their page, `https://graph.facebook.com/globalgroundslax/picture?type=large`. This resolved to a Facebook CDN URL serving their profile picture, which is a close-up of the actual "global Grounds COFFEE" storefront sign.

| File | Role |
|---|---|
| `images/storefront_sign.jpg` (and .webp) | Used as the Visit page hero background and the site favicon. Upscaled 2x from 480 to 960 wide. |

## From Restaurantji composite

Source: `https://cdn6.localdatacdn.com/images/3389711/d_global_grounds_coffee_photo.jpg`. Restaurantji publishes a 1400x500 composite of four real Global Grounds photos. Split into individual panels:

| File | What it shows |
|---|---|
| `images/exterior.jpg` (+ .webp) | Left panel. Brick facade, the storefront window, a customer visible through the glass. |
| `images/interior_chairs.jpg` (+ .webp) | Middle panel. Leather armchairs by the State Street window, a warm pendant lamp, plants, afternoon light. |
| `images/logo_mug.jpg` (+ .webp) | Top-right panel. A cream ceramic mug with the actual Global Grounds script logo on it, filled with dried flowers. |
| `images/food_cup_bowl.jpg` (+ .webp) | Bottom-right panel. A takeaway coffee cup next to a smoothie bowl. |
